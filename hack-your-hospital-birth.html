Key Deliverables

What you'll walk
away with

This isn't a standard antenatal class. It's an honest, practical deep-dive into the hospital birth experience — so you walk in prepared, not anxious.

1
Your birth plan

A clear, practical birth plan — used as a communication tool between you and your medical team, not a rigid script.

2
Know your rights

Informed and confident about your rights and responsibilities in the hospital environment — no more nodding along when you're unsure.

3
A partner who shows up

Your partner leaves knowing exactly what is expected of them when it matters most — with practical tools and the confidence to use them.

4
A toolkit for minimal intervention

Physical and emotional techniques to help manage labour — especially if you want to minimise medical intervention.

Inside the Workshop

Three hours of
real information

We cover what hospital birth really looks like — honestly and without fluff — so you're not surprised when it matters.

🏥

The Hospital Environment

What to expect when you arrive, who you'll encounter, how triage works, and how to navigate the system calmly from the moment you walk through the door.

📋

Birth Plan as a Tool

How to write a birth plan that actually works — as a communication aid with your care team, not a wishlist that gets ignored.

⚖️

Your Rights in the System

Informed consent, your right to ask questions, delayed cord clamping, skin-to-skin, and how to advocate for yourself in a busy hospital ward.

💪

Pain Management Options

An honest overview of both medical and non-medical pain management — epidural, gas and air, TENS, movement, water, breathing — and when to use what.

👫

The Partner's Role

Practical coaching for partners — how to read the room, what to say (and not say), how to advocate, and how to stay calm and useful throughout.

🔄

When Plans Change

What happens if labour is induced, if a caesarean becomes necessary, or if things don't go to plan — and how to stay grounded when they don't.
