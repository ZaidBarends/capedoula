What happens next

After you
reach out

1
Free consultation

We have a relaxed 30-minute chat — in person or virtual — to discuss your birth preferences, answer your questions and see if we're a good fit. No pressure, no commitment.

2
Booking & prep

If you'd like to go ahead, we formalise the booking and schedule your prenatal visit to build your birth plan together and go through everything you need to feel ready.

3
I'm on call for you

From that point, I'm available by WhatsApp throughout your pregnancy — and on call 24/7 from when labour begins until after your baby is born and you're settled.
